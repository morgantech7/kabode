About Us

Empowered Consumerism is a humanitarian NGO committed to improving the welfare of disadvantaged families through charity work, emergency relief, education support, healthcare initiatives and community development.

Mission

Core Values

Integrity, Transparency, Accountability, Compassion and Community Service.
